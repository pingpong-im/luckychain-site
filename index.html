01 01Key features

LuckyChain is the first comprehensive and decentralized ecosystem for online game software development for business, as well as legal and technical framework for new games and a marketplace for game developers. We are aimed to establish a unified system for all game participants with a mutually beneficial conditions

  • DAO protocol for online games software solutions for business
  • platform for tournaments and championships
  • game/smart contracts marketplace for game developers
  • framework for investments in game projects, players and teams
  • platform for crowdfunding and token sale for game developers

02 02 Challenges of online gaming
software for business

Time Costs
The average time to create ready-to-use business is about 4-5 months from the start of development process Existing platforms offer turnkey solution from $50,000 without value added services
Brand safety issues Technical risks
Most of the companies now are keen on saving their reputation and cannot rely on non-promising schemes. In case of fraud they potentially can possess serious reputational risks Any digital system is vulnerable to misconduct and could run out of control. Also, there is a chance that the platform can be hacked by criminals
Lack of comprehensive
product
The analysis of our team shows that there is no such a product to include every aspect for development of ready-to-use business. We suggest that such a product should at least include game development, game strategy elaboration, user acquisition, license issues and full-cycle support

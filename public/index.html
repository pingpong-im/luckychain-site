Our life is riddled with uncertainties

The lack of transparency leads to the situation when you can not be sure that competition is fair and nobody will benefit at your expense.

We believe that the use of the blockchain technology can be very helpful in solving this problem, so we decided to create LuckyChain.

Most common cases

Any rating systems based on votes made by ordinary people

Lotteries and jackpots

Housing and utilities and any other services that we don’t trust at this time

Other cases when we rely on third party and can be deceived

Product origins in local shops

Solution: Trust Protocol

Trust protocol is a high-level protocol based on the blockchain technology

In the trust protocol, any interaction between participants can be seen as a flat and fair.

As we cultivate fairplay among everyone, we intend to create such a decentralized protocol that eliminates any chance of unfair winning.

For instance, the owner of the movie rating website cannot cheat over the potential viewers because all the ratings are stored on the blockchain.

The LuckyChain trust protocol has several features:

Enables to interact for great number of participants

Flexible enough to be adopted to different competitive environments

Doesn’t rely on any central authority

Regular people have a single point of reference toward trust, and business

LC Core

The Core is the basic layer of ecosystem governance which includes upgradeable LuckyChain DAO, smart contracts to maintain registries of different participants, contracts for identity management and decentralized payment support. The LuckyChain ecosystem will support a range of identity solutions when they become production-ready.

  • LuckyChain DAO is responsible for:
  • Ecosystem governance;
  • Dispute resolution process;
  • Voting on upgrade proposals;
  • Distribution of the LuckyChain DAO token funds, including incentives and bounties for ecosystem participants;
  • Crowdfunding escrow for ecosystem participants.

LC dGames

On the basis of the two layers of LuckyChain architecture, game developers will be able to build any kind of dGames with integrated monetizations for use within the ecosystem.

Also, these dGames can be purchased by game operators, as well as the end users can have access to them, organize tournaments and even be consumers of streaming services. Overall underlying idea is to cover all aspects of potential gaming experience.

LC Infrastructure

The LuckyChain Infrastructure layers include all components required to establish a complete game development and monetizing cycle within the LuckyChain ecosystem, as well as create a marketplace for game developers and provide all other participants with useful tools for gaming experience.
